# AI Trend Radar Lab

A trend radar and learning lab for AI, cybersecurity, technical literacy and emerging signals before they become mainstream management topics.

This repository documents my learning curve, research notes, weak-signal observations and lightweight technical experiments around AI systems, cybersecurity, local AI, RAG, agents and organizational risk.

## Core idea

Technological change does not become relevant only when it reaches the mainstream.

By then, organizations are often already reacting.

This lab is a place to track early signals, understand technical concepts step by step and translate them into strategic relevance for organizations, communication systems, governance and adoption.

## Why this exists

I work at the intersection of AI adoption, UX, internal communication and governance.

To make AI systems operational, I need more than abstract trend awareness.

I need technical literacy, risk literacy and the ability to understand what emerging technologies could mean for real workflows, decision rights, trust, security and organizational behavior.

This repository documents that learning process in public.

## Role in the portfolio

This is the foresight and learning repository.

It tracks early signals in AI, cybersecurity, agents, local AI, RAG and technical literacy before they become mainstream management topics.

The goal is not to collect trends for their own sake, but to understand what they could mean for organizations, workflows, governance, communication and trust.

This repository is part of a four-repo portfolio system:

- `adoption-operating-system` — strategy, operating models and adoption logic
- `ai-governance-risk-toolkit` — trust, review, accountability and risk
- `ai-content-lab` — applied AI workflows for content and knowledge systems
- `ai-trend-radar-lab` — learning, foresight and early signals
